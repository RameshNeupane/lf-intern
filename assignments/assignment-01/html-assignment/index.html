Redmi Note 11 Pro 5G Launched in Nepal

Redmi recently added two new smartphones under the Note 11 series in Nepal. Among the two is the highly anticipated Note 11 5G, which is the successor to last year's Note 10 Pro. In this article, we will be discussing the following specs:

  • Design and Display
  • Performance and Memory
  • Camera
  • Others
  • Specification Summary
  • Price in Nepal

Design and Display

The Redmi Note 11 Pro 5G features a flat edge design, and it is 8.12mm thick and weighs 202 grams, the same as Redmi Note 11 Pro 4G. The phone is available in three color options: Polar White, Atlantic Blue, and Graphite Gray. As for the build quality, the back and front are glass, while the sides are aluminum.

Redmi Note 11 Pro 5G front features a 6.67-inch Full HD+ Super AMOLED display, which supports a variable 120 Hz refresh rate and 360Hz touch sampling rate. The screen can achieve a peak brightness of 1200 nits.

Performance and Memory

What separates the Note 11 Pro 5G from its 4G counterpart is the chipset. Redmi has included a 6nm-based Snapdragon 695 chipset, which is different from the Helio G96 found on the 4G variant.
Both 4G and 5G models of the Redmi Note 11 Pro are equipped with Xiaomi's LiquidCool technology. In terms of memory, Note 11 Pro can be configured with up to 8GB of LPDDR4X RAM and 128GB of internal storage.

Camera

At the back, Redmi Note Pro 5G has one less camera than its 4G counterpart. However, it won't be a major concern since it's a 2MP depth sensor that is missing. The rest of the camera setup is the same.Both get the same 108MP primary camera with ISOCELL HM2 sensor. The sensor combines 9 small pixels for a combined 2.1 m pixel. It is accompanied by an 8MP ultrawide camera and a 2MP macro sensor. There is a 16MP sensor on the front for selfies and video calls.

Others

Fueling the Redmi Note 11 Pro is a 5,000mAh battery with support for 67W turbo-charging. Xiaomi ships the phone with a compatible charger. The phone boots on Android 11-based MIUI 13. It has a stereo speaker setup and doesn't miss out on a 3.5mm headphone jack either.
